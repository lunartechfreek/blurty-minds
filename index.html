Welcome to BLURty Minds!

Are you ready to see how BLURty your mind is?

Here's how it works!

  • We will display a blurry picture, and its your job to guess what`s in the picture as quick as you can!
  • The timer will count back from 10 seconds, and with every second that passes the image will become clearer to make it easier to guess the answer!
  • Then the remaining time on the clock will be the amount of points scored, so the faster you guess, the higher you score!
  • For example if you guess the answer in a speedy 1 second you will score 9 points!
  • If you take 8 seconds to guess you will only score 2 points.
